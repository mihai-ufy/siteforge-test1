About Us

We started with a simple belief: building websites should be fast, fun, and accessible to everyone. Too many tools force you to choose between ease of use and flexibility.

SiteForge bridges that gap. Our visual editor lets anyone build beautiful pages, while the code escape hatch gives developers full control when they need it. Every site publishes to GitHub and can deploy to Vercel in one click.

Frequently asked questions

Can't find what you're looking for? Reach out to our support team.

What is SiteForge?

SiteForge is a website builder that lets you create, edit, and publish websites visually or with code.

Do I need to know how to code?

No! The visual editor is designed for everyone. But if you know code, you can use the code escape hatch for full control.

How do I publish my site?

Click the Publish button to push your site to GitHub. You can also deploy to Vercel with one click.

Is there a free plan?

Yes, you can start building for free. Paid plans unlock custom domains and advanced features.
